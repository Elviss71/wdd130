Mexico

The Oaxaca Temple, Mexico, is one of the temples constructed and operated by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, the 74th temple built by the church and the fifth one constructed in Mexico. It is located in the city of Oaxaca de Juárez. The Oaxaca Temple was built from white marble sourced from Torreón and features a classic design with a single spire. It has a total construction area of 994 square meters and includes two rooms for LDS ordinances and two sealing rooms. The Oaxaca Temple is used by more than 36,000 members from 10 stakes and affiliated districts of the church in the vicinity of the city of Oaxaca.
